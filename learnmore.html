COVID-19: Important information

Prevention & Control

We have always taken our standards of hygiene and cleanliness seriously, now more than ever in light of the current situation. We are training and educating our hotel team on housekeeping and hygiene protocols on a daily basis.

Reception

    We no longer accept cash payment at Milton's

    Online registration available to guests by e-mail before arrival.

    Detailed information for guests concerning COVID-19 measures at the hotel.

    Limitation of the number of guests at the reception.

    distancing in the reception area.

    Hand sanitiser at the property entrance and front desk or lobby area.

HouseKeeping

    Implementation of the new cleaning plans that involve disinfection of all surfaces and areas.

    Room linen will be changed on request (in case the guest does not want the room serviced during their stay).

    Room maids will wear gloves and masks

    All rooms will be cleaned with the windows and doors open to increase air circulation in order to ventilate the space.

    Special attention given to disinfecting frequently touched surfaces after cleaning (furniture, mini bar, light switches, telephone, hairdryer, safe, knobs, remote control, taps, pushbuttons, hangers etc.).

    Installed disinfectant gel dispensers in the different areas of the hotel, including public toilets used by guests and by staff, and other areas of interest.

Your safety is our priority - but you can help!

To help us keep you and our staff safe, please follow these guidelines:

    Wash your hands regularly with soap and water or use an alcohol-based disinfectant.

    When coughing and sneezing, cover your mouth and nose with your elbow or tissue paper hat you later discard into the trashcan and wash your hands.

    Avoid touching your face, mouth, nose and eyes. Avoid close contact with sick people who exhibit symptoms of fever, cough and / or shortness of breath.

    Avoid handshaking and close conversation - maintain social distancing (min 2 m).
